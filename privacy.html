Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2025

Introduction

Gunter Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Ballistic: Brick Breaker mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Information stored locally on your device:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • Purchased items and unlocked content
  • Game preferences and settings

Information collected by third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.
  • Apple Game Center: If you choose to sign in, Game Center collects your player ID for leaderboard functionality.
  • Apple StoreKit: Purchase history is managed by Apple for in-app purchases.

How We Use Information

We use the information to:

  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Provide leaderboard functionality through Game Center
  • Process in-app purchases through Apple

Data Storage

Your game data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store your personal game data on our servers. If you delete the app, your local game data will be lost.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The advertisements shown are configured to be appropriate for all audiences.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device's privacy settings
  • Choose not to sign in to Game Center
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
