Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option from the Shop. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all banner and interstitial ads.

My progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you deleted the app or switched devices, progress cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping your device backed up.

How does the prestige system work?

Once you reach a high enough lifetime score, you can prestige to reset your progress in exchange for permanent bonuses. Each prestige level gives you a 5% coin bonus.
